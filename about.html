About Us

Our Story

Whilst captaining the Ohio State Rugby team, Irish native Luke Hickey saw first hand the vast athletic potential bubbling below the surface in the rapidly growing United States rugby scene.

Inspired by the love for the game he found Stateside and its followers’ thirst for knowledge, Luke’s conversations back home with Connacht Rugby star and friend Eoin McKeon, quickly led to the formation, with group tour specialist Stephen Tummon, of Celtic Rugby Tours.

Celtic Rugby Tours delivers specifically tailored tours for High School and College Rugby programmes to specially selected rugby hotbeds in Ireland.

Each individually designed tour will allow visiting players and coaches up close and personal sessions with Irish professional rugby stars plus carefully selected games versus local clubs suited to your standard.

There’ll be plenty of chances to enjoy the famous Irish hospitality and “craic”, including attendance at either an Irish international fixture or another professional match and plenty of other activities led by Stephen who has brought over 200 successful tours to Ireland.

Team

Eoin McKeon

Eoin is a profesional rugby player primarily at number 8 for Connacht and Irish Wolfhounds.

Growing up in Galway he played his club rugby for Galwegians before signing with Connacht in 2009. Making his debut in April 2010. Since then Eoin has become a more and more prominent figure on the connacht side and has played in 5 Pro12 tournaments and two Heineken cup completions. He has been capped by Connacht over 70 times. Eoin brings a vast knowledge of rugby to our tours and gives all teams the very best advice to help them become better players.

Luke Hickey

Luke began his rugby career in Galway joining Corinthians at the age of 12. In 2007 when his family relocated to New Jersey Luke then joined Morristown Lions. Immediately breaking into the first team and captaining the side for his last two years. In 2010 Luke was enrolled at Ohio State University and started at scrum half for their senior team. Been giving the captains armband for the last two seasons showed how much Luke meant to the team. Currently Luke is playing with Columbus Rugby Club Ohio in the mens midwest premier league.

Stephen Tummon

An avid sportsman, Stephen competed successfully in many sports, before focusing on playing rugby with his school and basketball for Ireland. Stephen is an experienced tour specialist and has brought over and organised over 200 foreign tours to Ireland.
