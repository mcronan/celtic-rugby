Thanksgiving

Friday 18th November DUBLIN

Cead Mile Failte as you take your first steps on Irish soil. After arrival we will pass through the airport where you will be immediately met by your personal tour guide and driver for the week. It is straight into Dublin City for a Panoramic Bus Tour to point out the major city attractions. Then we will make our way to Aviva stadium to receive a private tour and learn about 150 years of Irish rugby history. We will arrive at the hotel for check in and have a welcoming dinner.

Saturday 19th November DUBLIN

The following morning after breakfast it is time to suit up and play our first Game. Today we will be playing against a local highschool team from Dublin on their home turf! After the first game it is into town for a well earned lunch followed by a tour of the Guinness Factory. Then it is up to the group whether they want to stay in town and explore Dublin or go back to the hotel for the evening.

Sunday 20st November CORK

The following morning following breakfast it is an early departure down to Glendalough to see Ireland's best glacial valley. It is a 6th century monastic settlement. Following glendalough it is time to hit the road and stopping at clonmel to enjoy lunch and a viewing of the Rock of Cashel. The last stop today is into blarney where will see blarney castle and kiss the blarney stone. Before checking into the hotel and having a team Dinner.

Monday 21nd November CORK

The following morning after breakfast it is time to suit up for another Game today we play a local highschool rugby team. Afterwards the team will be allowed to explore cork city on a Panoramic Bus Tour. The team will be given the afternoon to do what they want exploring the city. Before returning to the hotel on our bus.

Tues 22rd November GALWAY

Following breakfast the team will depart Cork and make their way up to the world Famous Cliffs of Moher. Before stopping in a small seaside town to enjoy a light lunch before making our way to Galway. Upon arriving in Galway they team will receive a Panoramic Bus Tour of Galway city before checking into the hotel and receiving Dinner.

Wednesday 23th November GALWAY

Today the team will be coached by Connacht players and analysis of their previous matches will be shown by expert analysis. The players will receive inside access to the connacht facilites and receive a talk on how rugby is played in Europe. After training if the team is brave enough they will go and jump of the blackrock tower in salthill into the Galway bay to help their muscles relax before the game tomorrow. They will go into Galway town for dinner and will get the bus back to the hotel.

Thursday 24th November GALWAY

Today the team will play a local high school team in the morning. Afterwards the team will spend the evening at their leisure and can watch a Connaught session. There will be a farewell Thanksgiving dinner in an American diner in Galway town that evening before going back to our hotel.

FRIDAY 25th

The team will wake early and hit the road to Dublin for their flight home.
